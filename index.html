Trails & Activities

🥾 John Muir Trail

Classic Kettle Moraine loops ranging 4–16 miles. Mix of prairie, forest, and kettles. Trailhead near Ottawa Lake.

🧊 Ice Age Trail

Designated National Scenic Trail tracing the edge of the last glacier. Multiple segments accessible from the Southern Unit.

🚵 Emma Carlin Trail

Mountain biking and hiking. About 12 miles of scenic wooded singletrack and doubletrack.

🛶 Ottawa Lake Canoe Trail

Paddling trail on Ottawa Lake. Canoe/kayak rentals available on-site.

🏊 Swimming

Swimming beach at Ottawa Lake and Whitewater Lake. Lifeguard hours vary — check at the park office.

🎿 McMiller Sports Center

Multi-use recreation area nearby. Archery, shooting sports, and more.

Good to Know

🚗

Vehicle Admission Pass Required

Every motor vehicle needs a pass. Buy online in advance or at the gate. Buy online →

🪵

Firewood — Don't Bring It From Home

Firewood must be from within 10 miles of the park, or DATCP-certified. Stops the spread of emerald ash borer. Buy it at the park or nearby.

📏

Site Driveway Max 30 ft

Sites 763 & 764 have a 30-foot max driveway. Plan trailer/RV lengths accordingly.

🕒

Check-in 3 PM / Check-out 1 PM

Don't show up at 9 AM expecting to set up. Plan a late lunch stop on the way if needed.
